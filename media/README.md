# Media Folder Structure

## Where to Store Your Photos and Videos

### Photo Folders
Place your photos in these folders:
- `public/media/photos/Tokyo/` - Tokyo photos
- `public/media/photos/Chennai/` - Chennai photos
- `public/media/photos/Coimbatore/` - Coimbatore photos

### Video Folders
Place your videos in these folders:
- `public/media/videos/Tokyo/` - Tokyo videos
- `public/media/videos/Chennai/` - Chennai videos
- `public/media/videos/Travel_Vlogs/` - Travel vlog videos

## File Formats
- **Photos**: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
- **Videos**: .mp4, .webm

## How It Works
The website will automatically detect and display files you add to these folders. Just drop your media files into the appropriate location folder!

## Example
To add a photo of Tokyo:
1. Navigate to `public/media/photos/Tokyo/`
2. Copy your photo file (e.g., `sunset.jpg`)
3. The photo will appear when you click the Tokyo folder on your website
